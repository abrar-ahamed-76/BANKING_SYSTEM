Account Management

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by going to your dashboard and clicking on the profile settings. Upload a new profile image if desired.

How do I change my password?

Navigate to the "Change Password" section in your dashboard. Enter your current password and choose a new secure password.

How do I add a new account?

Contact our customer support to add additional accounts to your profile. We can help you set up savings or checking accounts.

Transactions

How do I make a deposit?

Go to the Deposit section in your dashboard. Select your account, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction.

How do I transfer money between accounts?

Use the Transfer section. Select the source and destination accounts, enter the amount, and complete the transfer.

How long do transactions take to process?

Internal transfers are instant. External transfers may take 1-3 business days depending on the destination bank.

Cards & Payments

How do I apply for a credit card?

Visit the Credit Cards section and fill out the application form. We'll review your application and get back to you.

How do I pay bills online?

Use the Bill Payments section. Select the bill type, enter the bill details, and make the payment securely.

What if I lose my card?

Contact us immediately at 1-800-SBI-HELP. We'll help you block the card and issue a replacement.

Loans & Savings

How do I apply for a loan?

Go to the Loans section and complete the application form. We'll review your application based on your credit history.

What are fixed deposits?

Fixed deposits are savings accounts with higher interest rates for a fixed term. Apply in the Fixed Deposits section.

How do I start investing?

Explore our investment options in the Investments section. We offer mutual funds, stocks, and bonds.

Security

How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

What should I do if I suspect fraud?

Contact us immediately. We'll investigate and help secure your account.

How do I enable two-factor authentication?

Two-factor authentication can be enabled in your account settings for added security.

Troubleshooting

I'm having trouble logging in

Check your username and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the reset option on the login page.

My transaction failed

Ensure you have sufficient funds and that all details are correct. Contact support if the issue persists.

The website is not loading

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem continues, contact our technical support.
